Web Design vs. Developement:
------------------------------------
    - web designer : creates the overall look and feel of a website
    - web developers: implement the design using HTML, CSS and JS code



Why good design is important:
-----------------------------
    - Creates an immeditate and lasting good impression of the brand 
        or product 
    - Makes the user trust the brand right away;
    - Increase the user's perceived value of the brand or product;
    - Gives users exactly what they were looking for when coming to the 
        site, e.g. purchasing a product or finding information



ingredients of web design:
--------------------------
    01) typography
    02) colors
    03) Images/Illustration 
    04) Icons
    05) shadows
    06) Border-radius
    07) Whitespace
    08) Visual Hierarchy
    09) User Experience 
    10) Components/Layout:
    
Note: Design decisions for each ingredients are based on web personality



Overview of Website personality:
--------------------------------
    01) Serious/ Elegant:
        - for luxury and elegence, based on thin serif typefaces, golden
          or pastel colors, and big high quality images

    02) MInimal/Simple:
        - Focusses on the essential text content, using small or meduim 
          sized sans-serif black text, lines and few images and icons 

    03) Plain/Neutral:
        - Design that gets out of tech way by using neutran and small 
        typefaces and a very structured layout. Common in big corporations

    04) Bold/Confident:
        - makes an impact, by featuring big and bold typography paired with
          confident use of big and bright colored blocks

    05) Calm/ Peaceful:
        - for products and services that care, transmitted by calming pastel
          colors, soft serif heading, and matching images/illustrations

    06) Startup/Upbeat:
        - Widely used in startups, featuring medium-sized sans-serif 
          typeface, light-grey text and backgrounds, and rounded elements

    07) Playful/Fun:
        - colorfult and round desing, fuled by creative element like hand
            drawn icons or illustartion, animations, and fun langauage
